I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Linguistics at Faculty of Arts Chulalongkorn University in Bangkok, Thailand. My work is about Natural Language Processing (NLP), the technology that enables computers to understand languages and perform langugage tasks like answering questions, translating texts, and reading a large amount of text. My current research interests are Thai core NLP infrastructure, discourse phenomena, and NLP in data science and text analytics.

You can call me Te or อรรถพล. Thamrongrattanarit is my Thai last name. Rutherford is the last name I use in the US and in the academic world. This is confusing, I know.
